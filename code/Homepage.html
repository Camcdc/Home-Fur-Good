Welcome to Makhanda SPCA

Our mission is to ensure that every animal finds a home where it will be treated with care and dignity. We strive to provide a safe haven for animals in need, offering them love and support until they find their forever homes.

Animals are not just pets; they are companions that enrich our lives with their unconditional love and loyalty. By adopting, you not only save a life but also gain a loyal friend who will bring joy and companionship to your life.

Animals also deserve a chance to live free from cruelty and neglect. By reporting abuse, you play a crucial role in protecting these innocent beings and ensuring they receive the care and respect they deserve.

Our Mission

Our mission is to leverage innovative information systems to support the SPCA's vital mission of protecting and advocating for animals. We aim to develop a user-friendly, secure, and efficient digital platform that enhances public engagement, simplifies cruelty reporting, streamlines shelter management, and improves transparency in animal welfare operations. Through this project, we strive to contribute to a more humane society where technology empowers people to make a difference.!
